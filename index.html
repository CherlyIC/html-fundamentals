Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Cocoa powder
  • Baking powder
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Oil
  • Vanilla extract

How to prepare a chocolate cake

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Grease and flour a 9x13 inch baking pan.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, and salt.
  4. Add the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract. Mix until smooth.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared pan.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Allow the cake to cool before frosting or serving.
